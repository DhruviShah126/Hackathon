Royal Palm in 1901

In 1896-1897, Tuttle agreed to give Flagler some land so he could build his Royal Palm Hotel. With the construction of the hotel and a new railway station, the city of Miami began to grow into the metropolis it is today. In 1926 a hurricane severely damaged the hotel since it was constructed out of wood and not able to handle the strong winds. The hotel closed in 1928 after selling some of the hotel’s property to the city which greatly affected hotel management.

Royal Palm in 1927

Royal Palm was one of the first hotels in the city of Miami, and there are more and more every day. Because of this hotel, tourism sparked and flourished in South Florida. Now, Miami is one of the most visited cities in the United States.
